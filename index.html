Our Process

Every stitch
tells a story.

01
Source & Select

We source only full-grain hides — the highest quality cut, where the natural grain is preserved intact. No corrections, no coatings, no compromises.

02
Cut by Hand

Each pattern is cut by hand using steel rule dies, ensuring precision and minimal wastage. No shortcuts, no automation. Every piece is deliberate.

03
Stitch & Burnish

Saddle-stitched with waxed linen thread. Edges are burnished by hand and finished with natural beeswax — the way it has been done for centuries.

What We Use

Materials

01
🐂

Full Grain Leather

The strongest, most durable layer of the hide. It develops a rich patina with age, making each piece uniquely yours. The only leather that actually improves with use.

02
🪡

Waxed Linen Thread

Our saddle stitching uses hand-waxed linen thread — stronger than machine polyester. Even if one stitch breaks, the rest hold. Built to outlast machine lockstitch.

03
🔩

Solid Brass Hardware

All buckles, rivets, and closures are solid brass — rust-proof and developing a natural patina that matches the leather as your piece ages. Nothing chrome-plated.
