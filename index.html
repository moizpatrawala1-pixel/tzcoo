Our Process

Every stitch
tells a story.

01

Source & Select

We source only full-grain hides — the highest quality cut, where the natural grain is preserved intact. No corrections, no coatings.

02

Cut by Hand

Each pattern is cut by hand using steel rule dies, ensuring precision and minimal wastage. No shortcuts, no automation.

03

Stitch & Burnish

Saddle-stitched with waxed linen thread. Edges are burnished by hand and finished with natural beeswax for water resistance.

LEATHER
Quality is not a compromise we make. It is the only reason we make anything at all.

What We Use

MATERIALS

🐂

Full Grain Leather

The strongest, most durable layer of the hide. It develops a rich patina with age, making each piece uniquely yours over time. The only leather that actually improves with use.

🪡

Waxed Linen Thread

Our saddle stitching uses hand-waxed linen thread, stronger than machine polyester stitching. Even if one stitch breaks, the rest hold — unlike machine lockstitch.

🔩

Solid Brass Hardware

All buckles, rivets, and closures are solid brass — rust-proof and developing a natural patina that matches the leather aging of your piece. Nothing chrome-plated.
